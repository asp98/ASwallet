FAQ

How do I top up my balance?
Go to the Top-Up menu, choose a payment method, and follow the transfer instructions. Your balance will be updated automatically.
Are there any transfer fees?
Transfers between KopiWallet users are free. Other methods may have fees.
What if I forget my PIN?
Click "Forgot PIN" on the login screen and follow the instructions to reset it via email.
